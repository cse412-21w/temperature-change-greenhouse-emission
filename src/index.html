Temperature Change and Greenhouse Emission Over Years

GitHub Page

One of the most important issues the world and all world citizens face is the climate change in this technological era. The record of historical temperature change provides us the chance to show how it progresses over times at various time periods in different countries and also overall world temperature change. Carbon emission is one of the main causes of climate change that is worth more attention, so we want to show people the current situation to raise awareness and call for actions for possible reduction in carbon footage so that we can contribute as much as we can to save the world. Emission Of Greenhouse Gases

Carbon Dioxide, methane, and Nitrous oxide, are three major components for greenhouse gases. And they are the one of the significant causes of the global warming. Following graphs display the flunctuation of three gases during 1990 to 2018. We can observe that these three gases all have an incresing trend around year 2017.

Carbon Dioxide Emission And Temperature Changes of Each Country:

The following interactive graph shows the average emission of carbon dioxide from year 1990 to 2018, and the changes of temperature for each year. By using the selection bar, one could simply see the average emission of carbon dioxide through out the years and temperature changes of a particular country. It is notable that most of countries experienced a growth in temperature during these year.
